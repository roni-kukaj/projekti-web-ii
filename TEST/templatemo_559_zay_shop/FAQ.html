Orders & Returns

  • How do I place an Order?

  • Here are some steps you can follow:

    1. Go to the Shop page or search the product you want to buy. You can usually search for products by category, brand, or keyword.
    2. Select the product(s) you want to purchase and add them to your shopping cart. You can usually adjust the quantity of the product(s) in the shopping cart if needed.
    3. Once you have added all the products you want to purchase to your shopping cart, click on the checkout button.
    4. Enter your personal and shipping information, including your name, address, email and phone number.
    5. Select your preferred payment method and enter your payment details.
    6. Click on the submit or place order button to complete your purchase.

  • How Can I Cancel Or Change My Order?

  • Our shop does not allow cancellations or changes after the order has been processed or shipped. You may need to wait until you receive the order and then follow our return or exchange policy.

  • How Can I Return a Product?

  • Here are some steps you can follow:

    1. Contact our customer service as soon as possible to initiate the return process. You can find our contact information on the website or in the order confirmation email.
    2. Provide the customer service representative with your order number and explain why you want to return the product. Be specific about the reason for the return, such as a defect or an incorrect item.
    3. Follow the instructions provided by the customer service representative. They may ask you to fill out a return request form, or they may provide you with a shipping label and return instructions.
    4. Once we receive the returned product and verify its condition, we will provide you with a refund or exchange.
